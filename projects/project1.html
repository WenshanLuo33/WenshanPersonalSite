Mobile Brickman

A home designed for mobile workers

As Yangzhou expands, urban demolition generates **recyclable bricks**. These bricks, once discarded, are collected and repurposed by **"Brickmen"**, who make a living by sorting and selling them.

This project introduces a **mobile, balloon-based structure**, enabling brick collectors to **live, work, and sort materials efficiently**. Designed for adaptability, the structure allows them to relocate as new demolition sites emerge.

The goal is to create a sustainable cycle—reducing waste, repurposing materials, and providing housing solutions for transient workers.

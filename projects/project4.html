Mobile Brickman

A home designed for mobile workers

The project in Glasgow’s Yorkhill area examines student enclaves and their impact on local communities.

It proposes a network of public spaces, improved streets, and green corridors to enhance student-local interactions.

Centralizing student housing aims to create a more inclusive and sustainable community.
